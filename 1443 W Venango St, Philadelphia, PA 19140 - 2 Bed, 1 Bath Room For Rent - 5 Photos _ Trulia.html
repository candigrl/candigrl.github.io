Description

Convenient location for Temple medical students and staffs. Rent includes all the utilities and high-speed internet wifi. This is a room with a shared bathroom (with female Temple Univ. Student) and shared kitchen. Students preferred or people with proof of income and good credit required.

The house is located right next the Temple Lewis Katz Medical Education and Research Building (MERB). It is very convenient for Medical School students, residents, fellows and visitors to have a conformable place to live and short walk to Medical School and Gym. Quick and easy access to all types of public transportation including Temple University shuttle bus, riding to Center City only about 15 minutes. - newly remodeled kitchen, bedroom and bathroom - Central Air Conditioning - Video Surveillance Security System and Smart Home Smoke/CO Detector - Keyless Apartment Entry - Hardwood floors - New Appliances (Refrigerator, Oven, DW, Microwave, W/D) - Granite countertop - WIFI/Cable Available - Onsite Landry Price include: - 1 month security deposit - WiFi/Water/Electricity/Gas included in the base rent
